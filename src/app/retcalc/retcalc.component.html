Where do these numbers come from?

Your predicted value takes into account your income, monthly contributions, and current savings balance in order to predict where your account will be by the time you reach your preferred retirement age. The rate of return is default set to 5%, but can be changed. The rate of return on investment fluctuates, but it's best to set it lower than it is currently. This calculator also takes into account a 1% raise from year to year based on your income.

As for the needed account value, the calculator utilizes the "80% rule". This means that it calculates how much you will need for retirement as 80% of your current annual salary. It assumes that you need roughly 80% of your current annual salary per each year of retirement. There are actually many ways to figure out how much you need for retirement, but this rule is a good rough estimate to get started.
